Bengaluru

Experience the vibrant culture, historical richness and stunning gardens of Bengaluru, the 'Garden City of India'

Top three activities to do at Bengaluru

Elevated Oasis: Nandi Hills

The hills are known for their scenic beauty and rich history, and are a popular destination for hiking and rock climbing

Nature's Haven: Lalbagh Botanical Garden

The garden is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike, offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city and an opportunity to explore nature and learn about horticulture

Bustling Nightlife

Visitors can find a range of experiences, from casual pubs to high-end lounges, live music venues, and rooftop bars
